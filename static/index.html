About Our toDoBot

toDoBot is a Discord bot that can be used to schedule daily tasks. This taskBot offers many functionalities to help in scheduling tasks while helping you do many things with your tasks, like adding, deleting or editing them.
While using the commands may seem intmidating at first, it is easy to get used to them quickly! We hope to make the lives of our users easier with the convenience of our very own toDoBot!
